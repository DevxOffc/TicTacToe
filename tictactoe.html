Tic Tac Toe

Select a mode to start playing
Wins: 0 (You) - 0 (Computer)

How to Play:

  • 😏Easy Mode: 3×3 grid, align 3 boxes to win
  • 🙂Medium Mode: 5×5 grid, align 4 boxes to win
  • 😓Hard Mode: 7×7 grid, align 5 boxes to win
  • 🙃Pro Mode: 10×10 grid, align 4 boxes. Need 4 wins to complete the game
  • You play as X, computer plays as O

🤔Strategy Tip: In Pro mode, plan your moves carefully as boxes used in wins become unavailable for future wins!
